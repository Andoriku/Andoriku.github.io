Hello! My name is Andrew Jordan. I am a Designer, Developer, Artist, and Musician. I am from Asuncion, Paraguay but was raised in Milwaukee, WI. Attending Whitefish Bay High School set me up for success at MIAD (The Milwaukee Institute of Art and Design), in which I graduated with a B.F.A. in Industrial Design.

From there, I began my career at Steelcase Inc. As a part of the Global Design Studio, I worked on futures projects and an economy class of modular work desks. After Steelcase, I worked at Kohls Corporate as a floorplan management specialist. I have also worked at WeightUp Solutions and 42 Lounge. Currently I am a UI/UX Designer and Project manager at TSR AppLabs.

Now, as a student at devCodeCamp, I am starting the new chapter in my career. Learning full stack development, I am transitioning into the role of Jr. Developer at TSR. This site was actually created by myself. If you want to know more, just drop me a line in the contact section.
